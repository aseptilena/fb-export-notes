About Facebook Data Exporter

The original motiviation for this project was to preserve notes I've written about my kids. Facebook is a great platform, but I don't know if I will continue to have access to the notes I wrote last year in ten years time. In general, as we put more information online it becomes more important to have some sort of backup mechanism to ensure that the data is not bound exclusively to a single service provider.

About the author

I'm a thirty-something geek dad with two kids. I dabble in all sorts of things, including web services development (largely in Python).

FAQ

Does Facebook Data Exporter store my data?

Facebook Data Exporter does not store your notes, status messages, or links. Your content is simply retrieved from Facebook and returned to you in the format of your choice whenever you click one of the download links.
