Facebook Exporter

This application lets you export notes, status messages, and links from Facebook.

Author

Limitations

Due to limitations of the Facebook platform you will not be able to access data from before July 2008.

Note regarding "DownloadError" messages This project is hosted on Google App Engine. Occasionally, possibly due to strict timeouts, an attempt to export your data will fail with a "DownloadError" message. In general the error will go away if you reload the page.
